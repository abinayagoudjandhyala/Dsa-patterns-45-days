### Valid Palindrome

**Logic:**

* Use two pointers, one from the start and one from the end
* Skip non-alphanumeric characters
* Compare characters ignoring case
* If characters mismatch, it is not a palindrome
* Move both pointers inward until they cross

**Why it works:**
A palindrome reads the same from both ends, so comparing characters from left and right is sufficient.

---

### Reverse a String

**Logic:**

* Place one pointer at the start and one at the end
* Swap characters at both pointers
* Move pointers inward until they meet

**Why it works:**
Each swap puts characters into their correct reversed positions.
